About Angaza

Why we started Angaza

In Swahili, angaza means to illuminate — to shine light on something. That's what we set out to do: give Kenyan parents bright, CBC-aligned learning resources their children can hold in their hands, at a price that respects family budgets.

Every worksheet, story and flashcard is made with Kenyan names, places and everyday life, because children learn best when what they're reading looks like home.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

How do I receive my resources?

After you pay, your files are sent to the WhatsApp number you enter at checkout. Most orders arrive within minutes.

How do I pay?

Pay securely with M-Pesa. Send Money to the number shown at checkout, paste your confirmation code, and we do the rest.

Can I print the resources at home?

Yes. Every printable is A4-ready, so you can print at home or at any nearby cyber café.

What ages are the resources for?

Angaza covers Pre-Primary through upper primary — use the grade filter above the resource grid to find what fits your child.

Do you have free samples?

Yes — look for the green "Free" tag on resource cards above. Download, print, and try them with no payment needed.
