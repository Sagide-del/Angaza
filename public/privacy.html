Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026 · Angazakids, Nairobi, Kenya

1. Overview

This Policy explains what information Angaza (trading as Angazakids) collects when you use angazakids.co.ke, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We collect only what we need to process your order and deliver your files, in line with Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019.

2. Who this Site is for

Angaza sells learning materials for children, but the Site itself is intended to be used by parents, guardians and teachers to browse and purchase on a child's behalf. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children, and checkout always requires an adult's contact details (name, WhatsApp number).

3. Information we collect

  • Order details — your name, WhatsApp number, and optional email address, provided at checkout.
  • Payment confirmation — the M-Pesa confirmation code you enter (or, where STK push is used, payment status returned by Safaricom's Daraja API). We do not see or store your M-Pesa PIN or full M-Pesa account details.
  • Order contents — which activities you purchased, their price, and the date of purchase.
  • Basic technical data — standard web log information (such as browser type and pages visited) used only to keep the Site working properly.
  • Cart data — items in your shopping cart are kept temporarily in your browser and are not linked to you until you check out.

4. How we use your information

  • To verify payment and deliver your purchased files via WhatsApp.
  • To respond to support questions you send us.
  • To keep basic records of orders for accounting and dispute resolution.
  • To improve the Site and the activities we offer.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties, and we do not use it for advertising.

5. Who we share it with

We share the minimum necessary information with the service providers that make Angaza work: Safaricom (M-Pesa/Daraja) to process payment, WhatsApp/Meta or our SMS/WhatsApp provider to deliver your files and order confirmations, and our hosting and storage providers (Vercel and Supabase) to run the Site and store your files securely. These providers only receive what they need to perform their specific function and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

6. How long we keep it

We keep order records for as long as needed for accounting, tax, and dispute-resolution purposes, and delete data that is no longer needed unless we are required by law to retain it for longer.

7. Your rights

Under the Data Protection Act, 2019, you can ask us to show you the personal data we hold about you, correct it if it's wrong, or delete it where we're not legally required to keep it. To make a request, email hello@angaza.co.ke or message us on WhatsApp — we'll respond within a reasonable time.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures — including encrypted connections (HTTPS), access-controlled storage, and admin authentication — to protect the information we hold. No online service can guarantee perfect security, but we work to keep your data safe and limit access to what's strictly necessary.

9. Cookies and local storage

The Site uses minimal browser storage to remember items in your shopping cart while you browse — this stays on your device and is not used for tracking or advertising.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy as our Site or the law changes. The "Last updated" date above always reflects the current version.

11. Contact us

Questions about this Policy or your data? Reach us at hello@angaza.co.ke or WhatsApp +254 748 519 923.
